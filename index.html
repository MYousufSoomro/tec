About us

Top Engineering Construction Company (Former Baloch brothers & Company) was established in year 2003 as a general Civil, Mechanical and Maintenance services and building firm. Since then our primary focus has been MEP, Civil construction & Steel works. Through our extensive task history of constructing ventures we have developed a customer service business philosophy. Proactive and timely customer service is the backbone of our company. All employees, owner, office staff, project managers and field personnel aim to please. TOP ENGINEERING CONSTRUCTION (TEC) is actively involved building exclusive infrastructure projects all over the country. We have completed a number of contracts with various Multinational and local companies and individuals.
